About Me

Hi, I’m Martin Økter — a mechatronics researcher, engineer, and all-around tinkerer. I split my time between academia and industry, working at the intersection of robotics, autonomy, and advanced manufacturing.

At the University of Agder, I contribute to research in robotics and control systems, while at Hive Autonomy I help develop innovative robotic solutions for real-world applications.

Beyond my professional roles, I’m driven by curiosity. I love learning new things, whether it’s 3D printing, coding, or building robots from scratch. IceCube Solution is my personal playground for experimenting, creating, and sharing projects that connect technology with creativity.
